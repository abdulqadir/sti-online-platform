About Us

This project is a follow up to the Unite Ideas Challenge #SciTechMatcher

It is being implemented by members of the UN interagency task team on STI for the SDGs, and is coordinated by the United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs (UNDESA) and the United Nations Office of Information and Communications Technology (UNOICT).

The objective is to illustrate possible functionalities of the Online Platform for the Technology Facilitation Mechanism of the Sustainable Development Goals.

A gateway to information on existing Science Technology and Innovation initiatives, mechanisms and programs for Sustainable Development.
